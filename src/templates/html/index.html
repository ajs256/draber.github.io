Spelling Bee Assistant

  • Tweet

Spelling Bee Assistant is a bookmarklet for Spelling Bee, the New York Times’ popular word puzzle. A great game but sometimes a bit frustrating when you're stuck. If you don't make it to the end - and honestly, how often did you you see the Queen Bee screen come up - you'll have to wait until the next day to find out which words you’ve been missing.

If you play on a laptop or a desktop computer, the Spelling Bee Assistant is a great improvement for the game. It’s packed with tools that help you to succeed without spoiling the fun.

Drag the picture at the right to your browser’s bookmark menu. Next time you are playing, click on the bookmarklet to launch the assistant.

Spelling Bee Assistant is made for desktop computers and laptops with a sufficient screen size.

Features

  • Toolbar

    • Grab the assistant by the title to drag it anywhere inside the game area
    • Toggle dark mode
    • Display the options panel
    • Minimize the assistant

  • Score so far

    How far have you been moving ahead so far and long is the way to the top? How many points will you need to become Queen Bee? That's the sort of question you get answered here.

    • ✓ What you achieved
    • ? What you still can achieve
    • ∑ The sum thereof

  • Spoilers

    Are there more words with four or five letters? Is there’s another pangram hidden in the hive? Or at least a good long term that moves you to the next level? The Spoiler panel won’t tell you the words, but it will tell you, how many of each length still lay ahead of you.

  • Spill the beans

    This little face might know a thing or two that you don't, so watch it while you type!

    • 🙂 You’re on the right track
    • 🙁 You’re barking up the wrong tree
    • 😐 You didn't type anything yet

  • Steps to success

    This panel gives you a nice overview of the different ranks with the added bonus of revealing the road to Queen Bee. The tier that you’re currently in is highlighted.

  • Surrender

    When you're eventually out of words but die to know the solution, this button will end your curiosity! Use it wisely - there is no way back and you won't be crowned Queen Bee either!

    The words you didn’t find will be linked to the respective terms in Google, maybe this will help you next time!

  • Set-up

    If some of these features aren’t right for you, simply disable them in this panel.

What is a bookmarklet?

Bookmarklets are small pieces of code that can enhance the functionality of a website. They reside, just like regular bookmarks, in the browser’s bookmark menu. Unfortunately, there is no straightforward way to assign an icon to them.

Does it work everywhere?

Spelling Bee Assistant works only with modern browsers and has been built with laptops or desktop computers in mind. Installing bookmarklets on a phone or a tablet is rather complex but - according to Google - it appears to be feasible. It can't be installed on the NYT app, though.

Does it not spoil the game?

There are other tools with a similar purpose, which - according to this article - the Spelling Bee community seems to enjoy. The challenge stays the same and so does the joy. But now you’re having a little helper at hand when you're stuck in a corner.
